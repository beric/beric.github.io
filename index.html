Oldio - Audiobook Classics

Oldio is an app that makes it easy to listen to thousands of free classic audiobooks whenever and wherever you are.

I independently built Oldio in early 2018 as my first self-published app. I wanted an audiobook player with simple but powerful interface which was also free of ads, and that allowed you to stream or download audiobooks for offline playback. The app also includes some fun features like shake to reset sleep timer, and auto adjusting theme based on screen brightness.

Technologies:
Swift, AVFoundation, In-App Purchases, Realm, Core Motion, Universal Links

Proven Job Search

Proven Job Search is an app for job seekers that makes it easy to find and apply to local jobs.

As the sole mobile developer at Proven I was responsible for both the design and development of this app. It was a complete re-write of the Job Search app previously built for applying to jobs on Craigslist back in 2012. I was also responsible for building the backend API, and maintaining the Android version.

Technologies:
Objective-C, Core Data, Push Notifications, Facebook SDK

Proven Hiring for Employers

Proven Hiring for Employers is an applicant tracking app that lets employers post jobs to multiple job boards, review applications to those jobs and contact candidates all from their iPhone or iPad.

As the sole mobile developer at Proven I was responsible for both the design and development of this app. I was also responsible for building the backend API, and maintaining the Android version.

Technologies:
Objective-C, Braintree SDK, Push Notifications, MapKit, Core Location

Job Search

Job Search was the first app that let mobile users easily apply to jobs on Craigslist with their full resume.

At the time the app was launched the process of attaching a resume with a job application was rather cumbersome from a mobile device. To improve this process the app featured a built-in resume builder, flexible file importing & syncing, and an easy to use job search interface.

Technologies:
Objective-C, Core Data, SQLite, Core Location, Dropbox SDK, AddressBook
