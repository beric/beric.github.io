Oldio - Audiobook Classics

Oldio is an app that makes it easy to listen to thousands of free classic audiobooks whenever and wherever you are.

I built and self-published Oldio in early 2018. I wanted an audiobook player with simple but powerful interface which was also free of ads, and that allowed you to stream or download audiobooks for offline playback. The app also includes some fun features like shake to reset sleep timer, and auto adjusting theme based on screen brightness.

Technologies:
Swift, AVFoundation, In-App Purchases, Realm, Core Motion, Universal Links
